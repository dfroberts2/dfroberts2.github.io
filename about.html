As most developers jokingly put it, “Hello World!” Since most internet attention spans last less than 30 seconds, I’ll try to keep this brief.

I hail from the greater New York area, spending time residing upstate in the Hudson Valley, south in New Jersey and further out in Philadelphia. I like to put down my hometown just as the “Northeast.”

Like 20 something year olds, I hold a wealth of niche interests. I enjoy maintaining a balance of technical, concrete skills along with creative ones.

For years, I studied engineering and ended up in a data analysis position at Verizon after finishing college. On the side though, I enjoy a fulfilling life as an amateur musician, making new creations when I can find the time. If you’d like to hear a sample of my creations, feel free to listen to the embedded Soundcloud player to the right.

Because of this creative/technical balance, I felt studying web development would lead me into a happier career. I believe that when the scientific side meets with the free-thinking creative side, truly great things can occur.
